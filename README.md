# Free IB Science Tools

Student-friendly tools for **IB Chemistry** (and eventually also **IB Physics**).

This repository is a hub for free web tools designed by me to help students revise more effectively, handle calculations confidently, and prepare for assessments such as topic tests, Internal Assessments, and Extended Essays.

### What Should I Revise? - IB Self-Assessment Tool

A simple free tool for students to self-assess their knowledge of IB topics per subject (currently only Chemistry) and choose what to revise accordingly.
We (yes, I was a student, once, too) all know the feeling of having too many possible choices and being frozen in the decision 

**Useful for:**

- choosing what to revise first
- identifying weaker topics and subtopics
- preparing before topic tests or exams
- making revision sessions more targeted

**Tool:** [What Should I Revise?](https://interstellardrumbeat.github.io/what-should-i-revise/)  
**Repository:** [View reporistory](https://github.com/interstellardrumbeat/what-should-i-revise)  

## Why I Make These Tools

We (_yes, I was student once, too_) all know the feeling of being overwhelmed by the sheer amount of material, concepts and possibilities within complex subjects like Chemistry and Phyisics. 

These tools are designed to make the approach to IB sciences easier for everyone.

The goal is not to replace studying, textbooks, teachers, or tutors. The goal is to give students simple, focused tools that help them study and learn better.
